FAQ

Hello, what's happening here?
We are CHEFF and our mission is to help Cardano adoption.

By supporting our projects you help others developers to understand Cardano technology and to integrate in theirs mainstream platforms like for example wordpress and many others.

We believe the best to way to learn something is doing, all code we publish in our repository are public and can be reused or shared for free.

How can I mint a twitter?
You will need Nami Wallet and authenticate your user with us

Twitter allows devs to integrate with their platform using a API, you authenticate using the twitter and we have access to your tweets, we are not writing or storing this information, they are used to mainly verify who you are.

After you send funds to your Nami Wallet and authenticated on twitter you are ready to go, copy some of tweet you want to mint and press Mint button!.

How much will cost me mint a tweet?
The service is free of charges you only pay the Cardano fees

We only ask your delegation to CHEFF pool because this is the way to fund our operations, we are not selling code or asking you to pay extra fees for minting.
